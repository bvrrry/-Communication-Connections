Our Services

Comprehensive speech pathology support tailored to your child's unique needs

Speech & Articulation

Clear speech sound production

We help children develop clear, intelligible speech so they can be understood by family, friends, and teachers.

  • Speech sound assessments
  • Articulation therapy
  • Phonological processing
  • Childhood apraxia support

Language Development

Understanding and using language

Supporting children in developing both receptive and expressive language skills for learning and communication.

  • Vocabulary building
  • Grammar development
  • Comprehension skills
  • Narrative abilities

Literacy & Reading

Foundations for reading and writing

Building the phonological awareness and literacy skills children need for reading and writing success.

  • Phonological awareness
  • Letter-sound knowledge
  • Reading comprehension
  • Dyslexia support

Early Intervention (0-5 years)

Supporting communication from the start

Assessment and therapy for babies, toddlers, and preschoolers showing early signs of communication difficulties.

  • Late talking support
  • Play skills development
  • Pre-linguistic communication
  • Parent coaching

Social Communication

Building friendships and social skills

Supporting children who struggle with pragmatic language, perspective-taking, and social interaction.

  • Conversational skills
  • Non-verbal communication
  • Perspective-taking
  • Autism spectrum support

Feeding & Swallowing

Safe and enjoyable eating

Assessment and therapy for feeding difficulties, oral motor skills, and swallowing challenges.

  • Oral motor development
  • Sensory feeding issues
  • Picky eating support
  • Mealtime strategies

AAC & Alternative Communication

Every child deserves a voice

Assessment and support for augmentative and alternative communication systems including devices and apps.

  • AAC assessment
  • Device programming
  • Visual supports (PECS)
  • Family training

School Readiness & Support

Preparing for academic success

Developing the communication, language, and literacy skills needed for kindergarten and beyond.

  • School readiness skills
  • Classroom language support
  • Teacher collaboration
  • IEP planning
